About InAmigos

A Foundation Built
on Compassion

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization founded on 23 September 2020 by Govind Shukla. Headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, the foundation works across India to create lasting social impact through six major initiatives.

Our mission — "Uniting Minds for Change" — drives every action we take, from feeding underprivileged families to planting thousands of saplings for a greener tomorrow.

Why Our Work Matters

Every Action Creates
a Ripple of Change

  • Breaking the Cycle of Poverty
